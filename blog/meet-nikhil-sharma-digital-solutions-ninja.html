Meet Nikhil Sharma | Digital Solutions Ninja, Tech Innovator & AI Expert

Read Time
5 min Read

Originally from India, I've been calling Canada my home for over 14 years. My journey began in Halifax, Nova Scotia, where I earned my computer science degree from St. Mary's University. Since then, I've lived in Halifax, Prince Edward Island, and now proudly call New Brunswick my home, where I also invest in real estate as a homeowner.

With more than a decade of experience, I've led the development of multimillion-dollar healthcare AI systems and various custom software solutions. I founded DigiBenders, my digital agency, to help businesses harness technology, optimize their processes, and build meaningful digital experiences. At DigiBenders, we specialize in custom app development, SEO, digital marketing, and innovative AI-driven solutions.

Outside of tech, I'm an active investor, dabbling in stocks, cryptocurrencies, and trading strategies to broaden my financial insights. Sometimes I leverage AI tools and analytics to enhance my trading strategies, providing unique insights into market behaviors. I've also embraced a remote-first approach with DigiBenders, giving our team the freedom to explore the world. Traveling to new places and experiencing different cultures fuels creativity, sparking fresh ideas that translate into exceptional products.

Currently, I'm passionately working on exciting projects like Linkroo.ai, an AI-powered tool designed specifically for LinkedIn content creation - helping professionals, entrepreneurs, and content creators craft high-quality, humanized LinkedIn posts paired with scroll-stopping, attractive images. Another passion project is BuyCanada.ai, a platform dedicated to discovering Canadian-made alternatives to mainstream products, supporting local businesses and conscious consumerism. Additionally, I'm continually experimenting with various other AI tools, exploring how they can further streamline processes and unlock new potential.

When I'm not immersed in digital solutions, you might find me exploring exotic destinations, experimenting with cooking new cuisines or enjoying good whisky. For me, life is about balance- crafting innovative solutions, enjoying life's pleasures, and continuously discovering smarter ways to integrate tech into our daily routines.
